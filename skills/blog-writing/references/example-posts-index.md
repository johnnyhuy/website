- **Projects fail by default.** Someone needs to hold the full picture and push.
- **Apply skill to bottlenecks.** Not just backlog tickets.

## What's Next Examples

### Audit Pattern
> "Audit your last week. How many recovery activities hit all four dimensions? How many were just low-effort agitation? Replace one scrolling session with a 20-minute walk this week."

### Question Pattern
> "Next time you evaluate an AI coding tool, ask not just whether the model is good, but whether the interaction design preserves your ability to think."

### Action Pattern
> "Audit your current hiring process. What percentage of interview time evaluates non-technical capabilities? If it's less than 30%, you're probably hiring good engineers and wondering why you don't have great ones."

### Experiment Pattern
> "Try this for one week: Schedule two recovery anchors. Protect them like meetings. Notice the difference in energy by Friday."
